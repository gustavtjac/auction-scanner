# My Interests

This file tells Claude what to look for when evaluating auction lots.

## General rule

**If something has high resell potential, it is always worth including — regardless of category.** This includes things like designer items, collectibles (e.g. Royal Copenhagen), brand-name equipment, rare or high-demand goods, and anything that can clearly be flipped for a significant profit.

## Categories I want

- Laptops and computers (especially MacBook, ThinkPad, Dell XPS)
- Smartphones (iPhone, Samsung Galaxy)
- Cars and vehicles
- Power tools (Makita, DeWalt, Bosch, Milwaukee, Festool)
- Watches (wristwatches, smartwatches)
- Large format printers and office machines
- Shoes
- Gaming computers and accesories

## Categories I do NOT want

- Furniture
- Clothing and textiles
- Building materials (planks, bricks, plasterboard, etc.)
- Restaurant inventory (soya bowls, chopsticks, catering equipment)
- Weighted blankets and bedding
- Scrap
- Toys
